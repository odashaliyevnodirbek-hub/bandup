Everything you need to know

Can I cancel anytime? ▼
Yes, absolutely. You can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. You'll keep access until the end of your billing period with no extra charges.
Is the 7-day free trial really free? ▼
Yes! No credit card required to start. You get full Premium access for 7 days. You'll only be charged if you decide to continue after the trial ends.
How accurate is the AI band score prediction? ▼
Our AI model is trained on thousands of IELTS responses and provides estimates within ±0.5 band score of your actual result. It's a strong indicator but not a guarantee — real exams depend on many factors.
Do you offer student or group discounts? ▼
Yes! We offer 20% off for students with a valid student email, and group pricing for institutions and language schools. Contact us for details.
What payment methods do you accept? ▼
We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), PayPal, and Apple Pay / Google Pay through our secure Stripe payment system.
